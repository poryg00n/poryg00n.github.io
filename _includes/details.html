Important Things To Know

  • Cell phone service is EXTREMELY limited (basically non-existent).
  • We recommend downloading offline maps so you can navigate without cell phone service.

Getting Here

For those flying in, there are a few options.





Monterey Regional Airport
(MRY)

  • Approximately 45-60 minute drive to Big Sur
  • Limited airlines (Alaska, Allegiant, American, United)
  • There's a shuttle that goes between the Monterey, San Jose, and San Francisco airports.

San Jose International Airport
(SJC)

  • Approximately 2-2.5 hour drive to Big Sur
  • All major airlines
  • Larger rental car inventory than Monterey; rental car center is directly across from the terminals

Due to the remoteness of the location, there are limited transportation options in Big Sur.
